Objectives of Pan India Free GATE Training Programme.


The main objective of launching Pan India Free GATE Training Programme is to provide free Online GATE Training to the students of all the disciplines across the country by High Quality Faculty Members who have already secured good rank in GATE and have completed M. Tech/ M.S/Ph.D from IISC/IITs/NITs and having the teaching experience in Government Engineering Colleges for past 3 years.




Outcomes of Pan India Free GATE Training Programme:


This program is not beneficial for students only, but will also enhance the process of teaching & learning of faculty members. Since this is an open access platform, this program is not only for the students of TEQIP Funded institutes, rather it is open for all. TEQIP faculties are spread all over India, having knowledge of multiple languages, thus lectures will be provided in regional languages also.
